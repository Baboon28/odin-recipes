Soup Recipe

This webpage is dedicated to teach you how to make a tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • Cream
  • 4x Tomatos
  • Parsely
  • Cheese of your choice
  • 2x garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatos
  2. Blend the tomatos
  3. Add the cream to the blender
  4. Blend once again
  5. Add the parsely
  6. Add the garlic cloves
  7. Blend once again
  8. Add to a plate and enjoy
