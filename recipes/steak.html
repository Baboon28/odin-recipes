Steak Recipe

This page will showcase the Ingredients and steps neccessary to make a steak

Ingredients

  • ribeye steak
  • pepper and salt
  • 2x garlic cloves
  • rosemary
  • salted butter

Steps

  1. Heat the pan
  2. Add the steak to the pan
  3. Add the butter to the pan
  4. Add the rosemary
  5. Cook each side for 3 minutes
