Ramen

This page is will give a step by step guide on how to make ramen.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of water
  • 2x packet noodles
  • chicken breast
  • spices of your choices
  • chicken stock

Instructions

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Add the ramen packets to the water.
  3. Cut the vegetables
  4. Drain the water from the ramen
  5. Add the ramen to a bowl
  6. Addthe vegatbles to the bowl
  7. Add the spices to the bowl
  8. Mix it all
